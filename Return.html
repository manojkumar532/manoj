Returns and Refunds

  • Easy Returns: myntra offers an easy returns policy for most products, where you can request a return through the app or website if the product is defective, damaged, or not as expected. The return period varies depending on the product category.
  • Refunds: After the return is processed, customers receive refunds either in the form of myntra credits, bank transfers, or original payment methods.
  • Replacement: If a product is defective or damaged, myntra offers a replacement service.
