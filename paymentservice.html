payment services

  • Multiple Payment Options: myntra accepts various payment methods like debit/credit cards, net banking, UPI, wallets, and myntra Pay Later.
  • myntra Pay Later: A service that allows customers to buy products and pay for them later, either within 30 days or in easy installments.
  • EMI Options: myntra provides the option to buy products on EMI (Equated Monthly Installments) for eligible items, helping users manage larger purchases.
